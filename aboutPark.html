Our Climbing-Park is open monday to sunday, 9am to 8pm. You can drop by without reservation, but we recommend booking in advance. The price for half a day we charge 12€, whole day is 20€.We offer Group-pricing for groups of more than ten and for schoolclasses.

With all the necessary precautions in place for COVID-19, Climbing Park Buxtehude has all of the fun and adventure that you can ask for!
